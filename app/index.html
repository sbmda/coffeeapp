About CoffeeJournal

CoffeeJournal is an app (of sorts) designed and built by yours truly! The goal is to offer you a space to log in all your coffee endeavours, store them on your phone, and find them at the tip of your fingers should you ever wonder about where you had that really good coffee, that one time.

Take the time to explore it and let me know what features you'd like added to it! For questions, comments, and suggestions, text me!
